Logo Concepts

Each direction explores a different balance of geometry, motion and negative space — built to scale across products.

Construction & Geometry

A consistent geometry system keeps the monograms optically balanced. Curves are built on controlled radii, strokes align to a modular grid, and negative space is engineered for clarity.

What we optimize

  • Geometric balance & optical corrections
  • Proportional rhythm between stems and counters
  • Negative space for recognizability at 16–24px
  • Symbol integration for “P + L” clarity

Color & Variations

Recommended Direction

Concept 05 is our top pick. A graceful ribbon unites P and L into a modern, timeless symbol. A refined gradient adds depth while keeping the mark versatile from app icons to signage, capturing both innovation and precision.

Which logo works best for Pi Lab

A technology‑driven brand like Pi Lab needs an identity that feels instantly owned and adapts effortlessly across every touchpoint. Concept 05 does just that: a single fluid gesture merges P and L, and its premium gradient works beautifully on screens, stationery and signage alike.

  • Elegant ribbon form unifying P + L
  • Premium gradient and subtle depth for modern UI
  • Exceptional scalability from 16 px to signage
  • Distinctive and memorable silhouette
